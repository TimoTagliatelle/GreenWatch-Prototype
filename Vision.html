Our vision is a transparent and accountable automotive industry.

We believe that reliable CO₂ data must be accessible, comparable, and independently verified to enable informed decisions by consumers, policymakers, and investors.

The automotive industry plays a key role in global greenhouse gas emissions. However, emission data is often fragmented, inconsistent, or difficult to compare. GREEN WATCH was founded to close this gap by providing a standardized, independent CO₂ monitoring platform.

What makes GREEN WATCH different:

    #Independent data aggregation without industry funding
    #Transparent methodologies and open documentation
    #Clear year-to-year comparisons across manufacturers
    #Focus on long-term accountability rather than marketing claims
